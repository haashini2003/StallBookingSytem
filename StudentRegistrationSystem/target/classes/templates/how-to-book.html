Step by Step Guide

How to Book a Stall

Follow these simple steps to book a stall for your campus event, exhibition or business activity using our online system.

1. View Available Stalls

  1. Click on "Available Stalls" in the top menu or from the home page button.
  2. Check stall details such as location, capacity, time slots and hourly price.

2. Fill Booking Form

  1. Select a stall and click the "Book Now" button.
  2. Choose the required date, start time and end time.
  3. Enter your name / student ID and any additional notes.
  4. Click "Confirm Booking".

3. Confirmation

  1. After submission, you will see a confirmation message.
  2. The booking appears in the "My Bookings" page with status CONFIRMED (or PENDING based on your rules).

4. Payment (if applicable)

  1. Pay the stall fee at the campus finance office or via the method informed by admin.
  2. Show your booking ID during payment for verification.

5. On the Event Day

  • Arrive at least 15–30 minutes before your booked time.
  • Show your booking confirmation or student ID at the stall allocation desk.
  • Set up your stall and enjoy the event!
